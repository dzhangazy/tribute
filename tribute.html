Heath Ledger

About Heath Ledger

Heath Ledger (1979–2008) was an Australian actor and director, widely acclaimed for his diverse roles and his unforgettable performance as the Joker in The Dark Knight, which earned him a posthumous Academy Award.

Life Events

  1. 1979 – Born in Perth, Australia
  2. 1999 – Starred in 10 Things I Hate About You
  3. 2005 – Nominated for an Oscar for Brokeback Mountain
  4. 2008 – Passed away in New York City
  5. 2009 – Won posthumous Academy Award for The Dark Knight

Key Works

  • 10 Things I Hate About You (1999)
  • A Knight’s Tale (2001)
  • Brokeback Mountain (2005)
  • The Dark Knight (2008)
